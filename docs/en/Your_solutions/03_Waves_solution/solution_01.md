# 🌊 Section 3: Waves

## 1. Wave Properties

A sound wave in air has a frequency of **440 Hz**.  

- Speed of sound in air: **343 m/s**  
- Speed of sound in water: **1482 m/s**

Find the wavelength:
1. In air  
2. In water  

---

### 🧠 Solution

We use the wave equation:

v = fλ  

Rearrange to solve for wavelength:

λ = v / f  

---

### Wavelength in Air

λ_air = 343 / 440  

λ_air ≈ 0.78 m  

---

###  Wavelength in Water

λ_water = 1482 / 440  

λ_water ≈ 3.37 m  

---

### Final Answers

- Air: **0.78 m**  
- Water: **3.37 m**
