## 7. Standing Wave Modes

### Problem

A standing wave with **four antinodes** is produced on a string of length $L = 80 \text{ cm}$.

Find the **wavelength $\lambda$** of this wave.

---

### Solution

For a string fixed at both ends:

$$
L = \frac{n\lambda}{2}
$$

where $n$ is the number of antinodes.

---

### Step 1: Identify values

$$
n = 4, \quad L = 80 \text{ cm} = 0.8 \text{ m}
$$

---

### Step 2: Solve for wavelength

$$
\lambda = \frac{2L}{n}
$$

$$
\lambda = \frac{2 \times 0.8}{4}
$$

$$
\lambda = 0.4 \text{ m}
$$

---

### Final Answer

$$
\lambda = 0.4 \text{ m}
$$
