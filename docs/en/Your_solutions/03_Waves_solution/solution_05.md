## 5. Echo Ranging

### Problem

A person shouts towards a cliff and hears the echo **1 s** later.  
Speed of sound in air: **343 m/s**  

Find the distance to the cliff.

---

### Solution

The sound travels to the cliff and back, so the total distance is:

$$
\text{total distance} = v t
$$

But the one-way distance is half of this:

$$
d = \frac{vt}{2}
$$

---

### Step 1: Substitute values

$$
d = \frac{343 \times 1}{2}
$$

---

### Step 2: Calculate

$$
d = 171.5 \text{ m}
$$

---

### Final Answer

$$
d = 171.5 \text{ m}
$$
