## 4. Magnetic Torque — Solution

The magnetic torque on a current loop is:

$$
\tau = N I A B \sin\theta
$$

---

## 1. Given

- Dimensions: $10\,\text{cm} = 0.10\,\text{m}$, $5\,\text{cm} = 0.05\,\text{m}$
- $I = 2\,\text{A}$
- $B = 0.3\,\text{T}$
- $N = 1$ (single loop)

Area:
$$
A = (0.10)(0.05) = 0.005\,\text{m}^2
$$

---

## 2. Angle

The magnetic field is **parallel to the plane**, so it is **perpendicular to the normal** of the loop:

$$
\theta = 90^\circ \Rightarrow \sin\theta = 1
$$

---

## 3. Calculate Torque

$$
\tau = (1)(2)(0.005)(0.3)(1)
$$

$$
\tau = 0.003\,\text{N m}
$$

---

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{\tau = 3.0 \times 10^{-3}\,\text{N m}}
$$
