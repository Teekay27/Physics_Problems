## 7. Wavelength and Frequency — Solution

Given:
$$
\lambda = 550\,\text{nm} = 5.5 \times 10^{-7}\,\text{m}
$$

---

## 1. Color in Visible Spectrum

A wavelength of **550 nm** corresponds to:

$$
\boxed{\text{Green light}}
$$

---

## 2. Frequency

Use:
$$
f = \frac{c}{\lambda}
$$

Where:
- $c = 3 \times 10^8\,\text{m/s}$

---

### Calculation:

$$
f = \frac{3 \times 10^8}{5.5 \times 10^{-7}}
$$

$$
f \approx 5.45 \times 10^{14}\,\text{Hz}
$$

---

## Final Answers

- Color: $\boxed{\text{Green}}$
- Frequency:
$$
\boxed{f \approx 5.45 \times 10^{14}\,\text{Hz}}
$$
