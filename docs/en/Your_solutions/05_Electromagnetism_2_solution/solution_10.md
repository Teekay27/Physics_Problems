## 10. Speed of Light in Media — Solution

Use the relation:

$$
v = \frac{c}{n}
$$

---

## 1. Given

- $c = 3.0 \times 10^8\,\text{m/s}$
- $n = 2.42$

---

## 2. Calculate

$$
v = \frac{3.0 \times 10^8}{2.42}
$$

$$
v \approx 1.24 \times 10^8\,\text{m/s}
$$

---

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{v \approx 1.24 \times 10^8\,\text{m/s}}
$$
