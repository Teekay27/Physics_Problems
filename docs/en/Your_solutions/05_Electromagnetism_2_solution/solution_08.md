## 8. EM Spectrum — Solution

Order electromagnetic waves by **increasing wavelength** (shortest → longest):

$$
\boxed{
\text{Gamma rays} \;<\; \text{X-rays} \;<\; \text{Ultraviolet} \;<\; \text{Infrared} \;<\; \text{Microwaves} \;<\; \text{Radio waves}
}
$$

---

## Final Answer (in words)

Gamma rays → X-rays → Ultraviolet → Infrared → Microwaves → Radio waves
