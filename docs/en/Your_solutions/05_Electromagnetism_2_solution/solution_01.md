## 1. Gauss's Law — Solution

Using Gauss’s Law:

$$
\Phi_E = \frac{Q}{\varepsilon_0}
$$

---

## 1. Given

- $Q = 2\,\text{C}$
- $\varepsilon_0 = 8.85 \times 10^{-12}\,\text{F/m}$

---

## 2. Calculate Flux

$$
\Phi_E = \frac{2}{8.85 \times 10^{-12}}
$$

$$
\Phi_E \approx 2.26 \times 10^{11}\,\text{N m}^2/\text{C}
$$

---

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{\Phi_E \approx 2.26 \times 10^{11}\,\text{N m}^2/\text{C}}
$$

**Note:** The flux depends only on the enclosed charge, not on the radius.
