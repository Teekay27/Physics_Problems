## 8. Lorentz Force — Solution

Given:
- $q = 2 \times 10^{-19}\,\text{C}$
- $v = 1.0 \times 10^6\,\text{m/s}$
- $B = 0.5\,\text{T}$
- Motion is perpendicular to the field ($\sin\theta = 1$)

---

## 1. Lorentz Force Formula

$$
F = qvB
$$

---

## 2. Substitute Values

$$
F = (2 \times 10^{-19})(1.0 \times 10^6)(0.5)
$$

---

## 3. Calculate

$$
F = 1.0 \times 10^{-13}\,\text{N}
$$

---

## Final Answer

$$
\boxed{F = 1.0 \times 10^{-13}\,\text{N}}
$$
